Accessibility Statement

At Innate Family Chiropractic, we are committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to all users, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive and user-friendly experience for every visitor, regardless of technology or ability.

Our Commitment

We are actively working to improve the accessibility of our website and follow the relevant standards and guidelines, including:

  • The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA
  • The Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) digital accessibility principles

Accessibility Features

Our website includes, but is not limited to, the following features:

  • Clear, consistent navigation and layout
  • Descriptive alt text for images
  • Keyboard navigability
  • Sufficient contrast between text and background
  • Responsive design for mobile and screen readers

Ongoing Efforts

We continue to monitor our website and make improvements based on user feedback and accessibility best practices. Accessibility is an ongoing priority as part of our commitment to serving all members of our community.
