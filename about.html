OUR VISION

We envision a world where every man, woman, and child expresses their optimal God-given Innate potential. We have a vision of a community where proactive health care is the priority and drugs and surgery have become a last resort.

OUR MISSION

It is our mission to help develop a thriving Kansas City metro through educating, empowering, and delivering specific and scientific chiropractic care to connect men, women, and children to their God-given Innate potential.

Dr. Thomas Adams

Born and raised in the Quad Cities, Iowa, Dr. Thomas Adams decided to pursue his career in chiropractic after his own life and health changed dramatically. Once finally turning to chiropractic care, he was able to avoid low back surgery recommendations, get off previously prescribed medications, and really turn his life around.

After graduating from the University of Iowa, He attended Palmer College of Chiropractic in Davenport, IA. He is now excited to bring his chiropractic passion and serve in Kansas City and surrounding communities. He enjoys treating patients of all ages because he believes chiropractic is for everyone in all stages of life, but his true passion is working within the pediatric & prenatal community to help young kiddos develop optimally and naturally. Dr. Adams’s mission is to create generational change by serving his community in the healing art of chiropractic at the highest level.

He is a member of the International Chiropractic Association (ICA), International Chiropractic Pediatric Association (ICPA), and Pediatric Experience (PX). Dr. Thomas Adams is also certified in the Perinatal Webster Technique & specializes in extremity adjusting.

OUR PROCESS

  • DAY 1

    30-60 minutes, depending on the size of your family.

    For your first appointment, we’ll have you fill out some questions about your health history, including the physical, mental, emotional, and chemical stress in your life. Then we’ll sit down for a free consultation explaining what we do and answering any questions or concerns you might have. This is where we’ll determine if you’re a candidate for chiropractic care, and if we can provide the health answers you’re looking for.

    Next, we’ll begin your examination. This will consist of a neurological/orthopedic test, spinal x-rays, and a postural analysis. Posture provides a unique window into the nervous system. When your body is compensating for stress on certain nerves, it shows up in your posture.

  • DAY 2

    We’ll discuss the findings of your examination in detail to give you a clear understanding of what we do and how it will benefit your health specifically. Finally, it will be time for your first adjustment! Next, we’ll give you some time to recover in our resting room, followed by a thermographic scan to make sure your adjustment was effective.

    It’s important to remember that since each human is unique, we all heal and get stronger at different rates. But when we restore your body’s intended functions, with time and repetition, your natural healing processes can start to take place.
